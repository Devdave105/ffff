Our Story

Born From a
Passion for
Perfection

Eescents was founded on a single belief — that luxury fragrance should not be a privilege reserved for the few. Our founder spent years traveling through the heart of the Middle East, France, and Southeast Asia, learning the ancient art of perfumery from master perfumers.

What began as a passion became a house. Every Eescents fragrance is blended by hand, bottled with care, and shipped with intention. We source only the finest raw ingredients — aged oud, rose absolute, rare musk, saffron — and transform them into wearable art.

Today, Eescents serves thousands of discerning clients across Nigeria and beyond. We do not mass-produce. We craft. And we will never compromise on quality for the sake of quantity.
